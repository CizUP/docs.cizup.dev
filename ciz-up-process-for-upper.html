Ciz & Up Process for Upper

Finding CizUP

Step 1: Access the Ciz & Up Page

  • View active Ciz & Up events by clicking the 'Ciz & Up' tab on the Cizup home page.

Step 2: CizUP Selection

  • Click on the 'Let's up' button and choose one of the active Ciz & Up events in the time period suitable for you.

Upping

Step 3: Finding a Suitable CizUP with 'Find'

  • Use the 'Find' feature to find a JesUP at a time and date convenient for you.

Step 4: Match and Check-In

  • When the designated time for the CizUP you have matched with arrives and 'Check-In' is done, the CizUP page will be updated automatically.

Evaluation

Step 5: Go to Evaluation Page

Go to the evaluation page by pressing the 'Go Rate' button.

Step 6: Evaluation Criteria

  • Evaluate the viewer's performance with 'Yes' or 'No' options according to the set criteria.

Step 7: Add Rating and Comments

  • After evaluating each criterion, add your comments about the Cizzer.

Step 8: Completing the Assessment

  • Complete the evaluation process by clicking the 'Submit and Finish' button.

  • Before completing the evaluation, click 'Are you sure to submit?' Pay attention to the warning, because you cannot change your evaluation after this stage.
