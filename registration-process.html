Registration Process

If you wish to access the Cizup system, you must first register. Below are the steps on how to complete this process.

Step 1: Access to the Registration Form

  • Access the registration form by clicking on the "Register" link on the homepage or directly via this link.

Step 2: Entering Personal Information

  • Fill in the required personal information:

    1. Name and Surname

    2. Email

    3. Phone Number

    4. Birt Date

    5. Username

    6. Password

Step 3: Completing the Registration Process

  • Complete the process by pressing the "Register" button. After successful registration, the system automatically redirects to the login page.

Congratulations!

You now have a cizup account.

Support

If you encounter any problems during the registration process, you can get help at contact our support team.
