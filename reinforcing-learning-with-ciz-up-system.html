Reinforcing Learning with Ciz & Up System

As Cizup platform, we are determined to develop innovative methods that will make the learning process more interactive, supportive and efficient. In this context, we designed the 'Ciz & Up' system to enrich our users' Python learning experiences and create an interactive community.

Purpose of Ciz & Up System

The 'Ciz & Up' system was created to encourage mutual aid in education and active participation in the learning process. Our aim with this system is for users to interact with each other and strengthen both their learning and teaching experiences.

Benefits for Cizzers

  • Applying New Concepts: Those who watch can reinforce the concepts they have just learned in real time through an application.

  • Moving to the Next Chapter: A successful Ciz & Up activity allows the user to move on to the next learning phase.

  • Receiving Feedback: Viewers can see their strengths and areas they need to improve through evaluations given by upholders.

Benefits for Uppers

  • Topic Mastery: Uppers test and improve their own topic mastery when explaining a topic to others.

  • Peer-to-Peer Learning: Uppers can gain new perspectives by exchanging information with the peer-to-peer learning model.

  • Community Contribution: Uppers increase the overall knowledge of the community by contributing to someone else's learning journey.

Mutual Gain

  • The 'Ciz & Up' system ensures that both the ticker and the upper add value to each other's learning experiences. Cizsizlers gain hands-on experience that reinforces their knowledge and allows them to progress to more advanced levels, while uplayers develop their teaching and communication skills and deepen their own understanding.
